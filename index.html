About Besties

Discover the story behind the movement empowering girls worldwide

The Besties Community

Besties is a global movement for girls building a worldwide community where every girl finds her tribe, builds unshakeable confidence, and knows she absolutely belongs. Thousands of girls across the globe are part of our movement, from local chapters to online communities where they support each other, celebrate friendship, and chase their biggest dreams together. We're not just a brand—we're a collective of girls empowering each other to shine, changing what friendship and community mean for the next generation.

Our Mission

Build a global community of girls who lift each other up, celebrate who they truly are, and discover their unique power to change the world.
