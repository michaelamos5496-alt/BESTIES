About Besties

Our Story

What started as the Best Girlfriends Podcast, hosted by four young girls, has blossomed into something much bigger. What began as candid conversations about friendship, confidence, and big dreams has evolved into a thriving community and movement.

Besties is more than a podcast—it's a digital home for everything that matters to growing girls. We're creating a space where friendship is celebrated, confidence is built, and every girl knows she belongs.

Our Mission

To inspire girls to dream bigger, build confidence, celebrate friendship, and discover their unique shine.
